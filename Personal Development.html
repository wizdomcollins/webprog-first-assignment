Summary of the Twelve Pillars

Take action, achieve my goals, practice proper time management, surround myself with like-minded individuals who share my values, and become a lifelong learner are some of the valuable lessons I take away from these chapters.

Setting goals are crucial because it provides a benchmark for measuring my progress. Without clear goals, it's difficult to determine my success. Writing down my goals can enhance cognitive awareness and commitment. i will apply this technique in my everyday life starting with small goals that are steps in achieving my long term goal(s)

I will design my life's plan to avoid falling into the influence of those who don't align with my aspirations. Prioritize my desired activities, as time is a valuable resource that diminishes over time. Surround myself with winners, successful individuals who embody the values and skills I seek to acquire and develop.

Overall, these chapters offer practical advice and insights on personal growth, building strong relationships, effective time management, and achieving my financial success and if i follow these steps carefully I will be well on my way to success.
